My Trails

In the first few years that I lived here my dad suggested that we build our own trail in our woods. The first three years was very slow progress. We didn’t work on the trail much and only build a couple hundred feet of trail. The speed of trail building really picked up in 2019, and 2020. In 2019 my neighbor Connor and I started to work on the trail ourselves and made quite a bit of progress, finishing the loop around my house, and started the loop around his house. In 2020 over the time that school was closed because of Covid-19 Connor and I worked most days on the trail and quickly finished the trail around Connor’s house. Currently, we have four bridges, three jumps, two drops, one mobile jump, and lots of berms.
